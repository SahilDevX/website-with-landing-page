Your Privacy Matters

At Preplaced, our priority lies in the privacy of its visitors. The data that you provide is solely used for the purpose of website optimization and analysis. This standard data helps us understand the content sought, to create a frictionless browsing experience. This data is also helpful in resolving any bugs or issues, understanding user behavior, and improving the proficiency of the platform.

Data We Collect

The data collected at Preplaced include: full name, email address, telephone number, internet service provider (ISP), usage data, internet protocol (IP) addresses, language, device information, session duration, scroll-to-page interactions, clicks, browser type, country, date and time zone, mouse movements, interaction events, page events, layout details, positional information, and the number of clicks.

We use this data to identify trends, administer site data, understand user movements, and gather demographic information. This data is also used to verify your identity, streamline account maintenance, and enhance technical support.

Any data freely provided by the user would be collected, at the time of website usage. Obligatory data requested by the website is essential for accessing the services of the website (unless stated otherwise). In cases where certain data isn't mandatory, the users have the freedom to share/not share such data. As such, this doesn't affect the availability or functioning of the services provided by the Website.

Any kind of third-party personal data obtained, published, or shared through our platform, has its liability over the user, involved in such an act.
